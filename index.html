About Gaushala

Baba Barthhari ji Ashram Gaushala situated in Dholadaar Mountaines of Himachal Pradesh at village Kutheda Kherla near Pindi dass ashram Amb Distt.Una (HP). This land is a spiritual worship place of so many Sadhu Devotees that is Baba Surender Nath ji ,Baba Mast Nath ji and Baba Pindi Nath ji. This Gaushala has started on 2nd feb 2020 by some members of Rudrsansklp society of social Entrepreneurship and welfare registered under hp society act 2006 in 2012 for providing shelter to injured stray cattle from the surrounding people came from different areas of the society and contribute for this gaushala.
